MISSION AND VISION

We are SOLVING ANTIMICROBIAL RESISTANCE PROBLEM

A post-antibiotic era is approaching where people can die from simple infections no longer curable by antibiotics. In the USA and EU, antibiotic resistance is currently responsible for more than 23,000 and 33,000 deaths, respectively. Globally, antibiotic resistance claims the lives of over 700,000 people per year in 2019, roughly one a minute. However, it is getting much worse. In 2016, a review on antimicrobial resistance by the UK government showed that at the rate of annual deaths globally are expected to reach 10 million by 2050 or one person every three seconds, with an estimated global cost of $100 trillion cumulatively over next three decades.
